Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Mushrooms
  • Root vegetables
  • Cooking oil
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Fill the pot with 1/2 oil.
  2. Set the temperature to around 330 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Deep fry the shrimp for 3 minutes.
  4. Deep fry the vegetables for about 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve while hot.
