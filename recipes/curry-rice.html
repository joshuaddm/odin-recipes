Curry Rice Recipe

Description

Delicious Japanese chicken curry recipe for a weeknight dinner! Tender pieces of chicken, carrots, and potatoes cooked in a rich savory curry sauce, this Japanese version of curry is a must-have for your family meal. (taken from justonecookbook)

Ingredients

  • Japanese Curry Roux
  • Your choice of spice
  • Meat/Protein
  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Honey
  • Apples
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Start to cook rice.
  2. Cut the protein source of your choice, and vegetables into bite-sized chunks.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pot and add garlic and onion to add fragrance. Add vegetable chunks and chicken to fry.
  4. Add vegetable chunks and chicken to fry.
  5. Pour water into the pot and stir in the grated apple and honey.
  6. Skim the scum off occasionally.
  7. When the vegetable and chicken is cooked, add the curry roux and turn the heat off.
  8. Make the curry roux pieces dissolve in a ladle with pair of chopsticks, one by one.
  9. Serve with cooked rice.
